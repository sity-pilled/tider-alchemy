# Tider Alchemy: Maints Lube

[head=1]Tider Alchemy: Maints Lube[/head]
──────────────────────────────────────────
                                             [italic]By Devin Foster[/italic]

[head=3]Materials List:[/head]
[bullet/]1 Shaker
[bullet/]1 Reagent Grinder
[bullet/]1 Microwave
[bullet/]1 Electrolysis Unit
[bullet/]1 Bucket
[bullet/]1 Glass
[bullet/]1 Steel
[bullet/]75u Water
──────────────────────────────────────────
[bullet/]Step 1: Electrolyze 55 units of the water, and add it to the bucket.
[bullet/]Step 2: Grind the steel and glass together.
[bullet/]Step 3: Heat the mix until it forms fersilicite.
[bullet/]Step 4: Transfer the fersilicite into the bucket.
[bullet/]Step 5: Transfer the remaining 20 units of water into the bucket.
[bullet/]Step 6: [bold]Enjoy.[/bold]

[color=#a6a6a6]NOTE: If you are doing this multiple times, you will need more than one container, otherwise the heated container will automatically form the electrolyzed water into hydroxide.[/color]
