# Tider Alchemy: Two Ingredient Toxic Smoke

[head=1]Tider Alchemy: Two Ingredient Toxic Smoke[/head]
──────────────────────────────────────────
                                             [italic]By Devin Foster[/italic]

[head=3]Materials List:[/head]
[bullet/]5 Meat (cannot be human, dwarf, slime, or diona)
[bullet/]3 Bananas
[bullet/]1 Vial
[bullet/]1 Beaker (optional)
[bullet/]1 Centrifuge
[bullet/]1 Reagent Grinder
[bullet/]1 Hotplate (or microwave)
[bullet/]1 Electrolysis Unit
[bullet/]1 Bucket
[bullet/]1 Gas Mask
[bullet/]1 Gas Tank (varies on race)
──────────────────────────────────────────
[bullet/]Step 1: Grind the meat in the reagent grinder.
[bullet/]Step 2: Put the meat slurry into the electrolysis unit.
[bullet/]Step 3: Heat the meat slurry with the hotplate or microwave.
[bullet/]Step 4: Transfer the slurry into the bucket. If you are only using the vial, repeat steps 1 through 3 until you use all the meat.
[bullet/]Step 5: Juice the bananas in the reagent grinder.
[bullet/]Step 6: Put the banana juice into the centrifuge.
[bullet/]Step 7: Put on the gas mask and toggle your internals.
[bullet/]Step 8: Pour all of the centrifuged banana juice into the bucket.
[bullet/]Step 9: [bold]Enjoy.[/bold]
